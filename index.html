Chicken Soup with Vegetables

This is a healthy soup that's easy to make. It's also great for meal prepping.

Ingredients

1 lb chicken breast, boneless

cut into 1/2 inch pieces

1 onion

diced

3 carrots

sliced

3 celery stalks

sliced

1/2 cup of white wine

optional

Nutritional Information (per serving)

Calories

300

You

Average

Lowest

Instructions

Step 1: Prep Ingredients

Cut the chicken into cubes. Dice the onions, slice the celery and carrots.

Step 2: Cook Chicken

Heat a large pot over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until no longer pink.

Step 3: Add Vegetables

Add the onions, carrots and celery. Cook until vegetables are tender.
